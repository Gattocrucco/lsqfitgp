52# (1/x d/dx)^m (x^-v J_v(x)) = (-1)^m x^-(v+m) J_v+m(x) 

53# (Abramowitz and Stegun, p. 361, 9.1.30) 

54# --> 1/x d/dx (x^-v J_v(x)) = -x^-(v+1) J_v+1(x) 

55# --> d/dx (x^-v J_v(x)) = -x^-v J_v+1(x)

60# = -2^(v-1) √s^-(v+1) J_v+1(√s) =
